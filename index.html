What is Functional Medicine?

It is a way of identifying and addressing the root cause of your symptoms through specialty lab testing and consultation. We use optimal lab ranges instead of conventional lab ranges as a preventative and optimal approach to your health. This means that the reference ranges we use may vary from what your conventional provider uses.

After identification of the root cause (environmental toxins [mold, parabens, herbicides, BPA, heavy metals, etc.], adrenal dysfunction due to chronic stress, gut dysbiosis, SIBO, micronutrient deficiencies, food sensitivities, leaky gut, etc.), I will give your body the necessary tools it needs to heal itself.

These tools consist of lifestyle changes (prioritizing quality sleep, destressing techniques, diet changes, movement) and supplements as well as other modalities such as guidance/referrals for acupuncture, chiropractor, IV/IM therapy, peptide therapy, infrared sauna, cold therapy and/or PEMF therapies if necessary.

Who Would Benefit from Working with Us?

  • Someone who is committed to their health journey.
  • Someone who wants answers to their vague symptoms such as low energy, headaches, brain fog, acne, moodiness, irritability, irregular cycles, depression and anxiety-like feelings or "feeling low."
  • Someone who wants to understand their body and the ways they can optimize their health.
  • Someone who wants to be given natural tools to help improve their current health condition.
  • Someone who wants unhurried visits with their provider where they feel genuinely heard, are provided with detailed education including thorough interpretation of their labs and given a customized, comprehensive plan.
  • Someone who is feeling overwhelmed by all the "health and wellness" information found on social media and needs help streamlining to match their particular needs.
